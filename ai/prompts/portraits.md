# Portrait Prompts

Use SDXL portrait models. Avoid celebrity likeness, modern uniforms, fantasy armor, and sexualized framing.

## Bisono recruit

Poor Spanish tercio recruit, early modern 16th century, worn wool doublet, cheap morion helmet, tired face, muddy collar, damp camp light, realistic historical portrait, plain background.

## Veteran soldier

Scarred tercio veteran, weathered face, old cuirass, dark beard, powder smoke stains, mud and rain, hard eyes, realistic cinematic portrait, early modern Spanish infantry.

## Captain

Tercio captain, austere command presence, worn sash, morion held under arm, steel cuirass, campaign tent background, subdued honor, realistic renaissance portrait.

## Armorer

Early modern armorer, soot on hands, leather apron, helmets and pikes behind him, workshop candlelight, realistic portrait, non-fantasy.

## Field surgeon

Field surgeon in campaign hospital, blood-stained apron, bandages, herbs, worn tools, tired humane expression, realistic early modern portrait.

## Chaplain

Tercio chaplain, plain black cassock, small crucifix, campaign dust, candlelit tent, solemn face, realistic portrait.

## Vivandera / camp follower

Camp supplier woman, practical clothing, covered neckline, carrying bread and wineskin, muddy road beside military camp, non-sexualized, realistic historical portrait.

## Enemy scout

Enemy scout, wet cloak, wary expression, early modern border campaign, no modern gear, realistic portrait, low light.
