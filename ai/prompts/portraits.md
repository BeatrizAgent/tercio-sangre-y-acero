# Portrait Prompts

Manual ChatGPT prompt base for character portraits:

Half-length historical painterly portrait, early modern Spanish tercio setting, plausible face but not a real person, sober wool and linen clothing, iron or leather equipment where relevant, muted colors, clean readable shape, transparent or flat magenta background, no text, no insignia copied from real brands, no modern costume, no fantasy styling.

Use for:

- bisono recruit
- veteran soldier
- field surgeon
- armorer
- captain
- enemy scout
- vivandera
- chaplain

For grim scenes, keep the face expressive rather than graphic. Fear, fatigue, illness, hunger, and pain can be suggested through posture, lighting, and clothing condition.
