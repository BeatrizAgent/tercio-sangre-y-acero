# Scene Prompts

Manual ChatGPT prompt base for mission and event scenes:

Wide painterly browser RPG scene, early modern Spanish tercio era, historical architecture and equipment, mud, wet boots, powder smoke, pikes, arquebuses, morions, cuirasses, rough timber, candlelight or overcast daylight, premium composition, no modern objects, no fantasy elements, no readable text.

## Scene Uses

- barracks
- armory
- hospital
- night watch in rain
- muddy road patrol
- powder escort
- siege breach
- tavern duel

## Mature SFW Scenes

For harsh events, use indirect composition. Show smoke, silhouettes, covered forms, broken gear, stains, empty bowls, abandoned carts, tense faces, or distant aftermath. Do not show explicit gore. Mark linked data with `mature: true` and `presentation: "blurred"`.
