# Game Pillars

## Browser RPG First

The main product is a Next.js browser RPG. Normal HTML/CSS UI carries most gameplay. Canvas can come later for visual flavor only.

## Management Over Exploration

The player advances through barracks, training, armory, inventory, equipment, missions, reports, and hospital routes. There is no open-world navigation.

## Equipment and Training Progression

Stats and gear both matter. Pikes, arquebuses, morions, cuirasses, fatigue, and wounds affect mission outcomes.

## Reports Over Action Combat

Combat resolves automatically. The payoff is a short, grim campaign report and changed soldier state.

## Scarcity and Wounds

Coins, fatigue, wounds, honor, delayed pay, and rank pressure the player more than loot showers.

## Historical Flavor

Use Spanish tercio-inspired atmosphere without claiming strict historical simulation.
