# Scene Prompts

Use early modern military hardship. No fantasy, no modern weapons, no glossy heroic tone.

- Barracks: cramped tercio barracks, wet boots, pikes stacked by wall, cheap candles, muddy floor, tired soldiers, cinematic realistic scene.
- Armory: renaissance armory workshop, morions, cuirasses, pikes, arquebuses, leather straps, smoke-dark timber, cinematic realistic scene.
- Hospital: field hospital tent, straw beds, bandages, surgeon tools, rain outside, grim but non-graphic, cinematic realistic scene.
- Rainy night watch: tercio soldier on rainy night watch, pike in mud, lantern glow, powder smoke in distance, cinematic scene.
- Muddy patrol road: muddy road through hostile countryside, tercio patrol silhouettes, wet banners, overcast sky, realistic scene.
- Powder convoy: guarded powder convoy, barrels under canvas, arquebusiers, tense road, early modern campaign scene.
- Siege breach: distant siege breach, smoke, broken masonry, pikes ready, no gore focus, cinematic realistic scene.
- Tavern duel: low tavern interior, tense duel before blades drawn, soldiers and candles, historical early modern atmosphere, non-graphic.
