# Roadmap

## Phase 0: Web Scaffold

Create `/web` with Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind, Prisma schema, seed data, and local demo persistence.

## Phase 1: Soldier, Stats, Barracks

Make the soldier dashboard clear, robust, and data-driven.

## Phase 2: Inventory, Equipment, Armory

Improve item inspection, buy/sell feedback, and equipment rules.

## Phase 3: Training

Add clearer costs, fatigue pressure, and rank-gated drills.

## Phase 4: Missions and Resolver

Expand formulas, mission types, and risk/reward tuning.

Story mode lives under `/missions?mode=story` and follows the UX contract in
[`docs/story_mode.md`](story_mode.md): light visual novel flow, centered choices,
icon reward previews, and low friction after the early tutorial chapters.

## Phase 5: Reports, Wounds, Loot

Make reports richer and wounds more meaningful.

## Phase 6: Hospital and Rank Progression

Add recovery choices, delayed pay, and visible promotion moments.

## Phase 7: Canvas Visual Modules

Optional canvas flavor: avatar, banner, mission illustration, rain, powder smoke.

## Phase 8: Accounts, Companies, Rankings, PvP

Only after the single-player MVP is stable.
